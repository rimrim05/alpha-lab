# momentum_concentrated

Underreaction plus slow institutional flow-chasing keeps 12-1 winners drifting, and in
S&P 500 mega/large caps the long leg carries most of the implementable momentum premium;
the counterparty is anchored fundamental investors who sell winners too early and average
into losers. The strategy holds the top 20 members by 12-1 momentum, inverse-62d-vol
weighted, refreshed monthly in 4 weekly tranches to cut turnover and timing luck, then
vol-targets the book at 20% annualized (Barroso & Santa-Clara 2015; leverage capped at
2.0x, floored at 0.5x) with a hard de-lever to 1.0x when SPY trades below its 200d SMA,
because momentum crashes are post-bear, high-vol events (Daniel & Moskowitz 2016), the
one regime vol-scaling reliably de-levers (Cederburg et al. 2020 show momentum is where
vol management survives out of sample). Falsifier: a sustained forward stretch where the
top-20 12-1 winners underperform the index gross of costs (the premium arbitraged away or
crowded into reversal), or a momentum crash that arrives at LOW realized vol with SPY
above its 200d SMA: either would mean the mechanism or its crash hedge no longer holds.
