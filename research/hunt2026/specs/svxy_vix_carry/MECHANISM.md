# svxy_vix_carry — mechanism

Implied vol (VIX) exceeds subsequently realized vol in ~85% of months by 3-4 vol points because hedgers systematically overpay for crash insurance; the seller of that insurance earns a structural premium in exchange for bearing a real, occasionally realized left tail (Feb-2018 killed XIV; SVXY survived and deleveraged to -0.5x). The counterparty is the persistent bid for downside protection (capital that is mandated to hedge and cannot hold the tail) so the premium persists post-publication. This spec harvests it only when it is measurably there and the regime is calm: risk-ON (0.5 SVXY + 0.5 SPY) when VIX < 25 AND VIX > 10-day realized SPY vol (annualized, in VIX points); otherwise 100% BIL. The daily-close gate cuts the sustained-vol-regime losses but cannot act intraday, so overnight gap events are the accepted residual risk. Falsifier forward: a blind year where the gate is ON most days yet SVXY carry is flat-to-negative (VRP compressed, too many sellers), or repeated gap events that make the ON-state left tail dominate the carry, or the gate flipping >40 times/yr (regime signal has become noise).
