# Pre-registration — EWMA vs realized-window vol inside vol_managed_qqq (matched pair)

### EXP-2026-07-14-ewma-vol

**Hypothesis** (one falsifiable sentence, mechanism included):
Replacing the 21d rolling realized-vol estimator in vol_managed_qqq with a
memory-matched EWMA (com=10, the flat-window's center of mass) improves net returns,
because the rolling window carries a "ghost vol" artifact, a single spike day pins
leverage low for exactly 21 days and then exits the window as a cliff, while EWMA decays
the shock smoothly, releveraging earlier in recoveries without reacting slower to the
spike itself.

**Layer touched** (exactly one) + registered baseline:
Layer B: vol estimator only. Baseline = frozen specs/vol_managed_qqq/spec.py
(sigma_target 0.25, vol_lookback 21, tolerance_band 0.05, 2.0 cap), untouched. The
treatment recomputes weights through the IDENTICAL target/band/cap code path with only
`rets.rolling(21).std()` swapped for `rets.ewm(com=...).std()`. Structural gate: the
runner's re-implementation with the rolling estimator must reproduce the frozen spec's
weight series exactly (max abs diff < 1e-12) before any variant counts; recomputed
holdout Sharpe must match the published results JSON (tol 0.01).

**Alpha type tag**: estimator

**Expected result** (numeric, on which evaluator):
Rolling 12m windows / quarterly steps (walk_forward.rolling_windows) on the full
panel_2005 history; per-window paired delta (EWMA − realized) of window net return.
The two estimators correlate > 0.95 at matched memory, so expected effect is small:
matched-pair median 12m delta between +5 and +25 bps with win share 52–60%, most of the
edge concentrated in windows containing a vol-spike recovery (2009, 2020-H2); turnover
roughly unchanged (the 0.05 band absorbs small daily EWMA drift).

**Alternative result** (what the world looks like if the hypothesis is false):
|median 12m delta| < 10 bps and win share within 45–55%, estimator shape is immaterial
at 21d memory on one liquid asset (the tolerance band already eats the difference). Or
negative: the ghost-vol cliff is actually beneficial (slow releveraging avoids bear-market
rallies), realized wins.

**Registered variants** (3, all reported; the matched pair alone decides):
EWMA com ∈ {10 (MATCHED PAIR, decisive), 5 (fast context), 15.67 (λ=0.94 RiskMetrics

estimator families may be added after results are seen.

**Decisive statistic (pre-committed)**: matched pair (com=10) only:
median across shared 12m windows of paired net-return delta, plus window win share.
Verdict rule:
- "EWMA better" if median delta ≥ +10 bps AND win share ≥ 55%;
- "realized better" if median delta ≤ −10 bps AND win share ≤ 45%;
